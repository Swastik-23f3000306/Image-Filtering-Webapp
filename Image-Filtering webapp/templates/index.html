Filter Descriptions

Mean Filter: Averages neighboring pixels to smooth the image and reduce noise.


Gaussian Filter: Uses weighted averaging for smoother and more natural blurring.


Laplacian Filter: Detects rapid intensity changes to highlight edges and sharpen details.
